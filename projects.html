AI, Drones, and Disaster Impact Analysis

As a research assistant working under Dr. Kim and graduate student Matthew Angel, I analyze drone and aerial imagery to assess road damage and passability following hurricanes. My work supports AI model validation and disaster response workflows through imagery labeling, accuracy comparison between human and machine judgments, and the development of GIS and Python workflows for damage classification.

United Way Disaster Assistance Analysis

As a Disaster Division Intern with United Way of Southwest Virginia, I analyze spatial and temporal patterns of disaster assistance following Hurricane Helene (2024) and Winter Storm Jett (2025). I maintain and standardize case data, produce monthly and cumulative reports across multiple disaster grants, and create geospatial visualizations to examine case clustering, funding intensity, and recovery trends.

Hydrochemistry Analysis & Watershed Modeling

As a student researcher under Dr. Gannon, I study hydrochemical signatures of scarp and dip‑slope drainage systems in the Valley and Ridge region of Virginia. My research integrates automated watershed delineation, terrain analysis, and isotope flux modeling to better understand hydrologic processes and slope‑driven variations in runoff chemistry.

Water Quality & Macrophyte Monitoring

As a Water Quality Lab Technician in the Carey Lab, I support reservoir and watershed monitoring through field sampling, laboratory preparation, and ecological data analysis. This work includes seasonal macrophyte surveys at Falling Creek Reservoir, where I document species composition and phenology, map spatial distributions, and develop improved monitoring protocols. I also conduct chemistry runs and analyze water quality and ecological datasets in R and ArcGIS to support management recommendations.

Iliamna corei Restoration & Monitoring

As a Co‑Lead Researcher in the Restoration Ecology Lab, I support the conservation and recovery of Iliamna corei, a federally endangered Appalachian endemic. I lead fieldwork including habitat assessments and debris clearing, develop long‑term monitoring and restoration protocols, and help secure grant funding to implement conservation measures.

Past Research Projects
